Why is Understanding Market Price Swing Is So Important

You must comprehend this idea of how price moves in swings if you want to become a really excellent price action trader. This is especially true if you trade using a swing or trend strategy.

Because if you don't comprehend how pricing fluctuates in swings, you'll wind up doing the following:

➢ You'll place your trade at the exact incorrect time! For instance, if the market is in a slump, you would sell when it was just starting to rise! This is bad!

➢ Which indicates that you will be stopped out or that you need to place a significant stop loss. If you size your positions depending on the stop loss distance, a large stop loss does not necessarily translate into a large risk. However, if you don't, you are taking a big risk.

➢ If your stop loss is quite high, it will take some time for the market to move down before you can start making money on your trade.

It's not a great scenario to be in, in all honesty. Every trader wants their trades to start making money as soon as they place it. However, as far as we are aware, the market is not like that; occasionally, things like that do occur but often it doesn’t. That is how the market operates.

In an uptrend, you should therefore try to buy when the market is in downswing. In a downtrend you ought to be trying to sell at an upswing.
