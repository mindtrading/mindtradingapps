The Bullish Inside Bar pattern is a trading pattern that indicates the absence of a downward trend in the market and points towards an upward trend. This pattern is formed by two candlesticks. The first candlestick is a downward candlestick and the second candlestick is an upward candlestick which does not completely encompass the body of the first candlestick and stays between the opening and closing prices of the first candlestick.
