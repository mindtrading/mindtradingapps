What is Bullish Engulfing Pattern?

The bullish engulfing candle signals reversal of a downtrend and indicates a rise in buying pressure when it appears at the bottom of a downtrend.

This pattern reverses the ongoing trend as more buyers enter the market and move the prices up further.

The pattern involves two candles, with the second green candle that is completely engulfing the body of the previous red candle.

One should remember the below points when trading with the Bullish Engulfing pattern:

  • Prior trend: One should note that the prior trend is the downtrend
  • Pattern: The second candlestick should be bullish and engulfing the body of the first candlestick.
  • Stop loss: Stop loss can be placed below the low where the bullish engulfing pattern occurs.
  • Confirmation of the pattern: Also, don’t forget to confirm the signals given by this pattern with other technical indicators as we have to use the Relative Strength Index in our example.
